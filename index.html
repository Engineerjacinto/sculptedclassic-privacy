Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

Sculpted Classic ("the App") is developed and operated by Anthony Asare ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Authentication).

Fitness Data: Workout logs, exercise history, personal records, and fitness goals you enter into the App. This data is stored in your account and synced to our cloud database.

Nutrition Data: Meals, food items, macro and calorie data you log. Barcode scans are processed using the Open Food Facts and USDA FoodData Central databases. We do not store barcode images.

Body Metrics: Weight, body measurements, and progress data you voluntarily provide.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the App, including feature usage frequency and crash reports.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location data.
  • We do not access your camera roll or photo library (except when you explicitly choose to log a progress photo).
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.
  • We do not display advertisements in the App.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize your fitness and nutrition experience.
  • To generate AI-powered workout recommendations based on your goals and preferences.
  • To sync your data across devices via cloud storage.
  • To process subscription purchases through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat.
  • To improve the App based on aggregated, anonymous usage patterns.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Authentication and cloud database (Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management (Privacy Policy)
  • OpenAI — AI workout generation, processed via our secure backend (Privacy Policy)
  • Open Food Facts — Food barcode database (Terms)
  • USDA FoodData Central — Nutrition data (API Guide)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring only you can access your data. All API communications are encrypted via HTTPS. Authentication tokens are stored using encrypted device storage.

Subscription and Payment Data

All payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card number, bank account, or other payment details. Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. Upon request, we will delete your data within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and on this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
